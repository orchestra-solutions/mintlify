# Orchestra Solutions

<Note>
**Happy Holidays from the Orchestra team!** We hope your integrations are merry and your deployments are bright.
</Note>

Payment orchestration platform. With a single API integration, route payments across multiple providers worldwide, support regional payment methods, and meet compliance requirements without building separate integrations for each market and provider.

<Tip>
New to Orchestra? Start with our [Getting Started](/getting-started) guide to unwrap your first integration in minutes!
</Tip>

## Holiday checklist

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="1. Register for sandbox access" icon="candy-cane">
    Head to the [developer portal](https://portal.epaytools.com/) to create your free sandbox account. No credit card required!
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="2. Generate your API key" icon="key">
    Once registered, navigate to API Keys in your dashboard and generate your first key. Keep it safe like a precious gift!
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="3. Make your first charge" icon="bolt">
    Follow our [First Charge guide](/make-first-charge) to process a test payment in the sandbox environment.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="4. Go live!" icon="rocket">
    When you're ready, switch to production credentials and start processing real payments.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
